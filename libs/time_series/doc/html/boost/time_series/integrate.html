Function template integrate

boost::time_series::integrate — Calculates the integral of the series.

Synopsis

template<typename Series> 
  concepts::TimeSeries< Series const  >::value_type 
  integrate(Series const & series);

Description

The integral is calculated by multiplying each run's value by the run's length, summing all the results, and multiplying the sum by the series' discretization.

Parameters:
series

The input series.

Requires:

Given a variable x of the series' value_type and y of the series' length_type, the expression (x*y) must be convertible to value_type.

Given variables x and y of the series' value_type, the expression (x+y) must be convertible to value_type.

Given a variable x of the series' value_type and y of the series' discretization_type, the expression (x*y) must be convertible to value_type.

Returns:

A value_type holding the result of std::accumulate of the series, multiplied by the series discretization.

Notes:

Currently assumes the zeros of the series do not participate in the integral.
