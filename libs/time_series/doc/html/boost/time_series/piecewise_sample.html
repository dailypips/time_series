Function piecewise_sample

boost::time_series::piecewise_sample — Calculate a piecewise constant series by sampling one series at steps specified by a Range.

Synopsis

template<typename Series, typename Periods, typename Out> 
  ordered_inserter< Out > 
  piecewise_sample(Series const & series, Periods const & periods, 
                   ordered_inserter< Out > out);
template<typename Series, typename Periods> 
  piecewise_constant_series< typename concepts::TimeSeries< Series const  >::value_type typename concepts::TimeSeries< Series const  >::discretization_type, typename concepts::RandomAccessRange< Periods >::value_type > 
  piecewise_sample(Series const & series, Periods const & periods);

Description

Calculate a piecewise constant series by sampling one series at steps specified by a Range. The steps are specified with periods as follows:

  • [-inf,*boost::begin(periods))

  • [*boost::begin(periods),*(boost::begin(periods)+1))

  • [*(boost::begin(periods)+1),*(boost::begin(periods)+2))

  • ...

  • [*(boost::end(periods)-1),inf)

Parameters:
out

The inserter into which to write the resulting series.

periods

The steps at which to sample the series.

series

The series to sample.

Requires:

Series is a model of the TimeSeries concept.

Periods is a model of the RandomAccessRange concept.

Out is a model of the OrderedInserter concept.
